12‑Week Year Scorecard

The 12‑Week Year framework compresses annual goals into 12‑week cycles to drive focused execution and accountability. This digital scorecard reflects your default weekly plan and helps you track progress across key activities. Each column below represents a weekly habit; mark completed tasks, enter counts, and your score will update automatically.

Quarterly Goals

  • Hold 25 new open house events.
  • Host 25 open house sessions.
  • Sign one new listing each week.
  • Generate four new leads per week.
  • Convert four new leads per week.
  • Complete one CE course or Broker exam each week.
  • Sign three new listings each month.
  • Make 25 calls to new prospects every day.
  • Network and attend events regularly.

Weekly Actions

  • 50 new introductions.
  • 25 calls to new prospects.
  • Four open house events.
  • Ten new leads from open houses.
  • Five conversions.
  • Two follow‑ups.
  • One property tour.
