M2M App Planning Document

This document outlines the core functional modules for the Marching to More Solutions app【392972657872649†L1-L215】. Modules include:

  • 12‑Week Year Productivity System: goal tracking, scoreboards and accountability groups.
  • Seller Net Sheet Calculator: calculates seller proceeds and generates a PDF.
  • Rent vs Own Calculator: compares 15‑ and 30‑year mortgage scenarios for clients.
  • Multiple Offer Comparison: lets agents evaluate purchase offers side by side.
  • Hampton Roads Utility Guide: a database of utility providers by city.
  • Preferred Vendor Directory & Communication: lists recommended service providers with integrated calling/texting.
  • Inside‑Sales Call Tracking: logs calls, uses an auto dialer and analytics.
  • Dynamic Property Condition Worksheet: rates property condition on a 1–5 scale and outputs a PDF.
  • Agent Education Library: searchable articles, files, videos and an AI assistant for Q&A.
  • Zion AI/ML Voice Assistant: a voice‑activated tool to search MLS, schedule appointments and more.
  • CRM Integration & Lead Management: imports leads, schedules follow‑ups and automates tasks.
  • Team Collaboration & Communication: group chat, task boards, file sharing and meeting scheduler.
